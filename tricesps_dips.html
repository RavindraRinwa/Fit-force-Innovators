TRICEPS DIPS Exercise

Steps

    1.Skullcrusher: Lie on a bench, extend your arms over your head, and lower the weight toward your forehead. Perform 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps.

    2.Close-Grip Bench Press: Keep your hands close together (around 8-10 inches apart) while bench pressing.

    3.Triceps Dip: Use parallel bars or a sturdy surface to work your triceps.

    4.Dumbbell Overhead Triceps Extension: Extend your arms overhead and lower the weight behind your hetriceps-dips

Befits

    1.increase in overall arm strength, power and endurance

    2.Allows you to lift heavier during other lifts like bench presses and shoulder presses.

    3.Improves stability of the shoulders and elbow joints

    4.Great workout for tennis, golf, basketball or cricket players - a movement that needs the full motion of the arm muscles
